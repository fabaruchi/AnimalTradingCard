Pug

Pugs are one of the oldest breeds around. They originated in China around 400 B.C

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 39-52 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Habitat: Adapt to any kind of home; but too hot or cold temperatures are not suitable for the pug.
